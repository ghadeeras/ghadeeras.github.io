glTF Renderer (Wire Frames)

This is a clone of my other glTF rendering toy. The only difference is that it renders wire frames of the glTF models.

The idea is to keep hidden face removal working, and to remove lines between primitive shapes that are on the same plane.

This is done by performing two passes:

  • Render normals and depths to a textures
  • Use the texture to render the wire frames, by giving a dark shade for fragments adjacent to fragments with significantly different normals / depths.
