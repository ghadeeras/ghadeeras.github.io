Controls / Key Mappings
  • Mouse dragging to look around
  • Mouse wheel to change focal length
  • W to move forwards
  • S to move backwards

Press keys 0 to 9 to control the number of samples per pixel:

0 ➡ 20, 1 ➡ 21, 2 ➡ 22, ... 9 ➡ 29

Press ALT + keys 0 to 8 to control the number of averaging layers:

0 ➡ 20, 1 ➡ 21, 2 ➡ 22, ... 8 ➡ 28

Samples Per Pixel: 1

Averaging Layers Count: 1

FPS: 0
