Description

A tree is a classic example on fractals or models that could be constructed in a recursive way. This instance of that classic example comes with a twist though (literally).

One of the transformations you could apply on the tree model here is to twist it. The challenge with twists is that they are non-linear trasnformations that could not be represented by a matrix applied uniformally on all vertecies of the model. A twist matrix is calculated for each vertex position in the vertex shader, as well as another twist matrix for each vertext normal to obtain realisting shading.
